Portfolio Site

Front-End

I wanted to design something that felt familiar and interactive. After a number of ideas, I landed on mimicking the Xbox 360 dashboard's tile style GUI due to its a simple way to showcase games and ads with the tiles images. The modals were inspired by the guide when pressing the Xbox button. Interactivity and design were created with HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

Technologies

While building the website, I utilized Flask for the server and routing, Jinja to create templates for the modals, WTForms, email.message & google's gmail api for email inquiry
