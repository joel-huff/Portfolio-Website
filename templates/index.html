Joel D. Huff

My name is Joel, and I recently graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. I am based in the San Francisco Bay Area. After years of working in recruitment (political and healthcare), I decided my career trajectory was not quite where I was hoping to be. My desire to create something more tangible (in an electronics sense), overrode my passion of making cold calls and being hung up on. One morning, I decided spoke to myself "Why on Earth am I doing this?". I didn't have a good response, so I decided to return to school. During this time, I was promoted at work all while trying to learn a new skill, complete coursework, and fight off a caffeine addiction brought on by late nights at a glaringly bright computer screen. Now that I graduated, I am looking into full-stack and game-dev roles, with a focus on ML and LLM implementation.
